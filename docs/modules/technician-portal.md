## Technician Portal

The Technician Portal screens let field technicians see and work their assigned jobs from a simple, mobile-friendly view.

### What this screen is for

The Technician Portal focuses on what a technician needs today: their jobs, basic details, and simple actions.  
Each entry is a work order assigned to that technician.

### Who typically uses it

Technicians  
Field leads  

### Key things you can do

• View assigned work orders for a technician  
• See job details such as location, description, and schedule  
• Open a job to work on it and update progress  

### Create a new job for the portal

1. Create and assign a work order in **Work Orders** or **Dispatch**.  
2. Make sure the technician is assigned on the work order.  
3. The job will appear automatically in that technician’s portal views.  

### Layout overview

**Job list**  
Shows the technician’s current and upcoming work orders.  
Each row shows a short title, location, and status.

**Job detail pages**  
Open when a technician taps a job. They show the full description and any key fields needed in the field.

### Typical workflow

Supervisor assigns work to a technician  
→ Jobs appear in the Technician Portal  
→ Technician opens each job, does the work, and updates status  
→ Completion flows back into Work Orders and Dispatch views  

### Tips

• Keep job titles and descriptions short and clear so technicians can quickly understand what to do.  
• Avoid over-assigning; keep each technician’s portal list realistic for the day.  
• Encourage technicians to update status as they go so the back office has an accurate picture.
