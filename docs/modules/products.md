## Products

The Products screen lists the items you buy and use, acting as a simple catalog that links to vendors and purchasing.

### What this screen is for

The Products screen shows reusable product records such as parts or materials.  
Each row is a product with basic information and, where set up, a vendor connection.

### Who typically uses it

Procurement and purchasing staff  
Inventory managers  
Operations managers  

### Key things you can do

• View a list of products your organization uses  
• Search for products by name  
• Open a product to see more details and vendor links  
• Use products when building purchase orders and inventory items  

### Create a new product

1. From the left sidebar, go to **Products**.  
2. Click the **New Product** or **Create** button if it is available.  
3. Enter a clear product name that matches how you buy or reference the item.  
4. Select the main vendor if that option appears.  
5. Add any useful notes such as internal codes or packaging details.  
6. Click **Save**.

### Layout overview

**Header**  
Shows the page title and may include a create button and search box.

**Product table**  
Lists products with key columns such as name and vendor.  
Click a row to open product details.

**Product detail**  
Shows more information that helps you purchase and link items correctly.

### Typical workflow

Product created in Products  
→ Used on purchase orders  
→ Items received into Inventory  
→ Same product reused for future orders  

### Tips

• Keep product names consistent with what appears on vendor quotes or invoices.  
• Link each product to the correct vendor to simplify ordering.  
• Reuse products instead of creating near-duplicates.
